Log Cabin

Log cabins are perfect for off the grid living. Log homes and cabins are a sustainable choice. With proper sealing and maintenance, log homes can be highly energy efficient. A solid log wall offers more thermal mass capabilities than other building components do. Wood is a renewable resource.

Tiny Homes

Tiny homes are wonderful for off the grid living as it plays into the sustainability aspect. Finances and energy saving are easier with a tiny home as you have less to worry about. With a tiny home, you can have more property to grow gardens or raise livestock on as well! Make more out of less with a tiny home!

Trailer Homes

If you are someone who is always on the move, a trailer might be the best home for you. You can easily travel off the grid and park your trailer anywhere and set up your temporary home. Think of a trailer as an moveable tiny home! Once again, this home will allow you to save tons of money in the long run.

About Off the Grid

Living off the grid is a lifestyle revolving around sustainability and renewable energy. Everyone who lives off the grid has different views of the lifestyle, but for the most part, the focus is living a sustainable life, reducing the resources they consume, and in fact producing more resources than they use.
